# 🧠 Roadmap AI — Your AI Learning Coach

Roadmap AI is an AI-powered study companion that turns any topic into a personalized, structured learning roadmap. It combines dynamic roadmap generation, an AI tutor, gamified progress tracking, and smart scheduling to help learners stay consistent and motivated.


---

## ✨ Features

### 🏠 Home Dashboard
Enter any topic — *Machine Learning, Python, Web Development, JavaScript, Data Science, UI/UX Design, C++, Cyber Security* — and instantly generate a dynamic AI-curated learning path. The dashboard tracks your day streak, overall progress, skills currently learning, and badges earned, alongside a "Continue Learning" panel and AI-generated motivational quotes.

### 🎓 AI Teacher — Professor Sterling
A dedicated AI tutor built on the Gemini API that answers coding, educational, and roadmap-related queries. It comes with guardrails to stay focused strictly on technical learning, plus suggested prompts like *"Explain Object-Oriented Programming"* or *"Debug Python index error."*

### 📚 My Skills — Skills Portfolio & Achievements
Monitor course mastery, verified strengths, and identified weak areas based on quiz performance, alongside gamified achievement badges.

### 🕒 Study History & Timeline
Tracks total time studied, structural steps cleared, and active learning tracks, with a completion timeline log and micro-credentials system (e.g., the **Active Student Badge**, awarded after finishing all milestones of a roadmap with a perfect checkpoint score).

### 🗺️ Roadmaps
Each generated roadmap breaks a topic into sequential, unlockable milestones (e.g., *Syntax & Primitive Types → Complex Data Structures → OOP → Asynchronous Programming → Testing & Capstone Project*) with an interactive path view and detailed milestone checklists.

### ✅ Quizzes — Checkpoint Assessments Hub
Generates dynamic, 25-question conceptual and practical quizzes via server-side Gemini AI, tailored to your selected course track and milestone module.

### 📊 Progress & Analytics
Visualizes learning data with study hour trends (last 30 days), category allocation charts, and subject performance distribution — giving a clear picture of where your time and effort are going.

### 🔥 Streaks — Consistency & Streaks
A habit-building hub with a streak counter, monthly ledger calendar, streak shield protection, and milestone targets to keep learning consistent day to day.

### 🏆 Achievements & Micro-Certificates
Unlockable badges (Pioneer, Night Owl, Fast Learner, Quiz Whiz, JS Adventurer, Consistency King, Weakness Slayer, Specialization Master) tied to a mastery rank and gamified XP multiplier system.

### 📅 Study Planner Scheduler
Configure daily available study hours and get a mathematically generated weekly schedule that sequences roadmap milestones onto specific dates, complete with resource/workload analysis.

### 🔔 Study Reminders & Notifications
Configurable daily study alerts and real-time desktop notifications to prompt consistency, with adjustable reminder times.

### ⚙️ Settings & Customization
Personalize your profile name and daily study goal capacity.

### 🌗 Dark Mode
Full dark mode support across the entire app for comfortable studying at any hour.
